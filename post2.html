Name:

Dolly

Type:

Project

Creator:

Marat Gilyadzinov

Source of Image: Marat Gilyadzinov

Description:

A handmade dolly that will add to any part of your desk or sidetable.


Steps:

1. Chain 10 stitches.
2. Slip stitch into the first chain to join up the edges together.

For the lacework:
3. Chain 3 stitches and double crochet into each stich within the circle.
4. Join the round to completion and chain 4 stitches.
5. On the next round, double crochet and chain 1 stitch into every stitch.
6. Slip stitch to join, then chain 10 stitches.
7. Treble crochet into the 2nd stitch.
8. Chain 6, then treble crochet into the following stitch. Once this round is completed, there will be 12 round gaps

For finishing the edge of the dolly:
9. Join the round, chain 1 stitch, and single crochet into that stitch.
10. Chain 3 stitches and double crochet into the gap below the row.
11. Chain 2 stitches and double crochet into the same gap.
12. Slip stitch to join the ruffle, then single crochet 1 stitch.
13. Continue to chain the stitches and double crochet as done in the previous steps around the circle.
14. Once done with the circle, cut off the yarn and weave it into the tail.
