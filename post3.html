Name:

Double Crochet

Type:

Pattern

Creator:

Karina L (Admin)

Source of Image: Karina L

Description:

A staple in crocheting that looks harder then it actually is! Once you've learned this stitch, you'll be able to expand on what you can do.


Steps:

1. Create a chain of however many iterations you would want, for instance, you can chain 12.
2. Wrap one loop over your crochet hook, then wrap the yarn over the hook.
3. Insert your hook into the fourth chain from the hook. There should be 3 loops on your hook.
4. Yarn over the hook again.
5. Pull the hook through the chain, still having 3 loops.
6. Yarn over the hook and pull it through 2 of the loops. There should be 2 loops on the hook.
7. Yarn over once more and pull through the last 2 loops.
8. Continue through steps 2 - 7 until you finish that row.
9. To begin a new row, turn the work over and chain 3.
10. Yarn over the hook and double crochet into the 2nd stitch of the row.
11. Continue steps 2 - 7 for the rest of the 2nd row.
